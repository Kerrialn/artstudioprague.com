Purchase

To purchase any of the works shown on this website please send an email to info@artstudioprague.com, stating which artwork you are interested in.

Delivery can be made free of charge within the Prague area and installation can be made at a small additional cost.

Artworks can be packed and shipped worldwide, at the purchaser's expense. The necessary documentation is provided for importation.

If you wish to see the artworks in Prague, please send an email and appropriate arrangements for a personal viewing will be made.
